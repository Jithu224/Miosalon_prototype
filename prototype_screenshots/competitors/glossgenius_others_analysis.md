# Competitor Analysis: Salon Staff Commission & Salary Management

## Date: April 2026

---

## 1. GlossGenius (US Market, AI-Powered)

**Pricing:** $24/mo base (Standard) + $40/mo payroll add-on + $6/employee/month

### Payroll & Commission Features

**Commission Structures:**
- Fixed-rate commissions: flat percentage per employee across all services
- Tiered commissions by seniority: e.g., Associate stylists at 45%, Level 3+ stylists at 65%
- Tiered commissions by revenue: e.g., up to $2,000 services = 35%, $2,001-$5,000 = 45%, above $5,000 = 55%+
- Per-service and per-product commission rates that can differ by employee
- Gratuity (tips) automatically tracked and included in payroll

**AI-Powered Optimization (Gloss Insights):**
- Analyzes salon's real performance data and benchmarks against similar businesses
- Data-driven recommendations to optimize commission structures
- AI Analyst (launched 2026) answers natural language queries like "Who are my highest revenue-generating clients?"
- Commission structure refinement based on industry trends and comparable businesses

**Payroll Flow:**
1. Appointments are booked and completed through the platform
2. Commission rates (service + product) are auto-applied based on staff profiles
3. Tips/gratuity are recorded at POS checkout
4. Hours logged via integrated time-tracking (clock-in from app)
5. Commission report auto-populates in-app
6. Owner reviews and runs payroll -- system auto-calculates W-2 and 1099 pay
7. Federal, state, and local taxes auto-filed in all 50 US states
8. Direct deposit to employees

**Staff Management:**
- Color-coded appointment calendars per staff member
- Role-based access control (calendar, clients, payroll visibility)
- Team performance tracking and individual dashboards
- Staff can view their own commission breakdowns

### What GlossGenius Does Well
- End-to-end payroll integration (appointments -> commissions -> tax filing -> direct deposit)
- AI-driven insights for commission optimization
- Multi-tier commission flexibility (seniority-based AND revenue-based)
- Full US tax compliance with automatic filing
- Staff self-service view of earnings

### What GlossGenius Lacks
- India-specific features (no GST, no UPI, no Indian tax compliance)
- Expensive for Indian market ($40+$6/employee is significant)
- No salary advance management
- No attendance-linked deduction system common in Indian salons

## 2. SalonBoost (India Budget Market)

**Pricing:** Standard at Rs.799/mo | Premium at Rs.1,199/mo (no setup fees, no per-transaction charges)

### Payroll & Commission Features

**What SalonBoost DOES offer (contrary to initial PRD assumption):**
- Staff attendance tracking with manual marking system
- Daily, weekly, and monthly attendance summaries on a dashboard
- Downloadable payroll reports
- Salary and commission calculation based on attendance records
- Leave tracking (sick days, vacation, personal time off)
- Performance analytics to identify top performers

**However, payroll features are LIMITED to the Premium plan (Rs.1,199/mo).**
The Standard plan at Rs.799/mo includes billing, appointments, inventory, and staff management but NOT payroll/commission.

**What SalonBoost Does NOT Offer:**
- No automated tax filing
- No direct deposit / bank transfer integration
- No tiered or seniority-based commission structures
- No AI-driven optimization
- No salary advance management
- No payslip generation
- Commission calculation appears basic -- percentage-based, not multi-tier

### Key SalonBoost Differentiators
- GST billing support (India-specific)
- UPI payment integration
- WhatsApp automation for client communication (Premium)
- Very affordable pricing for Indian salons
- No booking commissions or hidden fees

### Assessment
SalonBoost provides basic attendance-to-payroll reporting but not a full payroll system. It is more of a "calculate and report" tool -- the actual salary payment is done manually by the owner. This is typical of budget Indian salon software where most payments to staff happen in cash.

## 3. Dingg (India Mobile-First, Multi-Location)

**Pricing:** Starts around $149/mo for multi-location (US pricing); India pricing is custom/undisclosed. Flat monthly fee, no per-booking commissions.

### Payroll & Commission Features

**Commission Management:**
- Automatic complex commission structure calculation
- Service commission and retail/product sales commission tracked separately
- Tip distribution calculated automatically
- Commission splits for services involving multiple stylists
- Stylist PIN verification at POS -- when staff complete a service, they enter their PIN and the system auto-calculates commission based on preset percentages and logs it against shift hours
- Real-time commission tracking visible to stylists

**Staff Management:**
- Shift timing management
- Attendance tracking linked to sales via POS
- Performance KPIs per team member
- Personalized goals based on experience and role
- Visual dashboards and progress reports
- Feedback session documentation
- Multi-location staff scheduling (view across all locations)
- Cross-location performance comparison
- Standardized roles and permissions

**Payroll Integration:**
- Payroll processing based on custom schedules
- Detailed earnings reports for tax purposes
- Connects directly with appointment booking, POS, and staff management
- No manual data entry needed for payroll calculation

### What Dingg Does Well
- PIN-based stylist verification at POS is unique -- ties services directly to staff for accurate commission
- Strong multi-location support with cross-site analytics
- Integrated POS-to-payroll pipeline
- Goal-based performance tracking per staff member
- Flat pricing with unlimited team members

### What Dingg Lacks
- No AI-driven commission optimization
- No automated tax filing (generates reports, owner files manually)
- No salary advance management
- No payslip generation feature mentioned
- India pricing is opaque / quote-based
- No seniority-tier commission automation (manual setup only)

## 4. Salonist (Global, Multi-Location)

**Pricing:** Not publicly disclosed; appears to be quote-based. Targets mid-to-large salon chains.

### Payroll & Commission Features

**Commission Auto-Calculation:**
- Commission is automatically calculated at the time of payment (real-time), not at end of pay cycle
- Commission profiles can be created per staff member
- Goals can be set for services, products, and packages
- When staff reach a specific sales/appointment goal, they earn a higher commission percentage above fixed allowance
- Tracks repeat appointment rates per staff member
- Revenue generated per technician reporting

**Staff Management:**
- Staff scheduling with shift tracking
- Role-based access control
- Attendance tracking with automatic payroll calculation
- Multi-location operations support
- POS integration for service tracking

**Goal-Based Commission System:**
- Build commission profiles for all staff members
- Set monthly targets for number of sales and appointments
- When targets are met, staff earn additional commission percentage
- This model encourages upselling and client retention

### What Salonist Does Well
- Real-time commission calculation at POS (not batch)
- Goal-based commission tiers that incentivize performance
- Multi-location support
- Integrated with POS, CRM, and inventory
- Staff can track their own progress toward goals

### What Salonist Lacks
- No AI-driven optimization
- No automated tax filing or direct deposit
- Commission system is goal-based only, not seniority/revenue-tier based
- No salary advance management
- No Indian tax/GST compliance specifically
- Pricing transparency is poor

## Key Lessons for MioSalon

### 1. Salary Advance Management is an Unserved Gap
None of the four competitors offer salary advance tracking/management. This is a common need in Indian salons where staff frequently request advances. MioSalon can differentiate by building advance request, approval, and auto-deduction workflows.

### 2. Real-Time Commission at POS is Table Stakes
Salonist's approach of calculating commission at time of payment (not end of month) is the right model. Dingg's PIN verification adds accountability. MioSalon should combine both: real-time commission calc with staff verification at checkout.

### 3. AI Optimization is a Premium Differentiator
Only GlossGenius offers AI-driven commission insights. For MioSalon, even basic data-driven suggestions ("Ravi's productivity increased 15% -- consider upgrading to Tier 2") would be a major differentiator in the Indian market where no competitor offers this.

### 4. Tiered Commission Structures Need Flexibility
GlossGenius shows the gold standard: seniority tiers AND revenue tiers AND per-service rates, all configurable per employee. MioSalon should support at minimum:
- Fixed percentage per staff
- Seniority-based tiers (Junior/Senior/Master)
- Revenue-based slabs
- Per-service overrides

### 5. India-Specific Payroll is Underserved
No competitor does full Indian payroll (PF, ESI, TDS, professional tax). SalonBoost only does basic attendance-to-salary calculation. Building even basic Indian statutory compliance would be a significant moat.

### 6. Payslip Generation is Rare
Only GlossGenius generates actual payslips. Indian labor law requires payslip issuance. MioSalon already has this in the prototype -- this is a competitive advantage.

### 7. Staff Self-Service is Expected
GlossGenius, Dingg, and Salonist all let staff view their own earnings/commissions. MioSalon must include a staff-facing view showing:
- Current month earnings breakdown
- Commission earned per service
- Attendance record
- Advance balance
- Payslip download

### 8. Pricing Strategy
- SalonBoost at Rs.1,199/mo with basic payroll sets the floor for India
- Dingg at $149/mo for multi-location sets the ceiling
- MioSalon should aim for Rs.1,500-2,500/mo range with full payroll features to be above SalonBoost but well below Dingg/GlossGenius pricing
